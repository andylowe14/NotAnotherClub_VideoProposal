Record-Breaking Kickstarter Campaign

View the campaign →

I created the promotional videos for Lazarus Press's book launch campaigns. These weren't just any book launches - we were crowdfunding translations of obscure 19th century occult texts. The challenge was making something incredibly niche compelling enough that complete strangers would open their wallets and back the project.

The videos had to capture the handmade, artisanal aesthetic that was crucial to the brand, using original book illustrations in a stop-motion style. The client was very determined to pursue this route as a key differentiator in a landscape that is quickly becoming overrun with obviously AI-generated, cheap and quick content. I also handled all the sound design and music arrangement - cutting together classical pieces to create an emotional journey, plus composing the dramatic organ theme for the first campaign's finale.

The results speak for themselves - both videos were central to campaigns that broke records for book launches on Kickstarter.

  • Campaign 1: Over 1,000 backers, £90,000 raised
  • Campaign 2: £75,000+ in the first 48 hours alone
  • Both campaigns were featured by Kickstarter as quality projects

Product Demonstration That Actually Works

Watch the video →

Natracare needed a 1-minute video demonstrating that their wipes actually dissolve like toilet paper. Sounds simple, but the challenge was making something that people would actually want to watch and share, not just skip past.

I kept it light-hearted and snappy, getting straight to the point while making the science visually engaging. The client wanted it to grab attention immediately and deliver the message clearly.

  • Widely shared across their social channels and embedded in multiple blog posts
  • The video successfully converted browsers into customers by clearly demonstrating the product benefit

Viral Social Media Content

A crypto wallet needed to announce their new app integration. Their brand is "Nautilus" so they wanted something with a submarine theme. I took old movie footage of a submarine, layered in modern UI elements, and created a reflection effect of their logo in the captain's mask.

The whole thing needed to feel both vintage and cutting-edge, matching their community's vibe perfectly.
